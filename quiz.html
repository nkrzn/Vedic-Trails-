A Darshan Consultation

Find your yatra.

In the Vedic tradition, no pilgrim chooses their journey alone. They sit with a teacher, who looks at where they are in life, and points to a path. We have made that conversation into five questions.

5 questions About 90 seconds No email required

Question One

What is calling you to this journey?

There is no wrong answer. The mountain knows why you are coming.

Question Two

Which deity feels nearest to you?

If many feel near — or none feels specific — that is also a clear answer.

Question Three

Where are you in your life?

Different journeys suit different seasons. The right yatra meets you where you are.

Question Four

How long can you walk this path?

Some pilgrimages are weekend retreats. Some take fifteen days. Both are sacred.

Question Five

Your comfort range, per person?

Every Vedic Trails journey is fully managed. This is about the experience you would like at the temples and in between.
