The Journey

He was born here. He grew up here. He left and never came back.

Krishna was born in a prison cell in Mathura to Devaki and Vasudeva, smuggled across the Yamuna in a basket during a storm, raised by Yashoda and Nanda in Gokul and Vrindavan, and left Braj forever when he was sixteen to kill his uncle Kansa and reclaim Mathura. He never returned. The Bhagavata Purana says that the Gopis — the cowherd women who loved him most — spent the rest of their lives in a state between grief and ecstasy, aware that he was everywhere and nowhere.

Vrindavan today is a city of 5,000 temples. Widows have traditionally come here to spend their remaining years in devotion — a tradition both moving and complex. The temples range from the 16th-century structures built by the six Goswamis sent by Chaitanya Mahaprabhu to find the sites of Krishna's childhood, to ISKCON's enormous new complex. Every style of Vaishnava devotion is practised here simultaneously.

Holi in Braj is the original Holi — the festival recreates Krishna and Radha's play with colours, and the most famous version at Barsana and Nandgaon (Lathmar Holi, where women beat men with sticks) draws visitors from around the world in March.

Day by Day

The Itinerary

01
Day 01

Arrive Mathura · Krishna Janmabhoomi

The birthplace

Arrive Mathura by train from Delhi (2 hours) or fly to Agra and drive (1 hour). Krishna Janmabhoomi — the prison cell where Krishna was born, now enclosed within a temple complex adjacent to the Shahi Eidgah mosque. The juxtaposition is historically significant and the atmosphere is charged. Evening aarti at the Yamuna ghats. Check into heritage hotel.

  • Mathura station or Agra airport pickup
  • Krishna Janmabhoomi darshan
  • Yamuna ghat aarti
  • Heritage hotel Mathura
  • Welcome dinner
02
Day 02

Vrindavan · The 5,000 Temples

The forest of love

Full day in Vrindavan. Banke Bihari Temple — the most beloved temple in Vrindavan, where the deity is said to be so captivating that the curtain is opened and closed frequently to prevent devotees from being overwhelmed. ISKCON Temple. Prem Mandir — built over 11 years from Italian marble, its interiors depicting scenes from Krishna's life in extraordinary detail. Nidhivan — the sacred grove where Krishna is said to perform the Raas Leela every night. The caretakers lock the gates at sundown and leave. No one stays inside.

Included Today
  • Banke Bihari Temple
  • ISKCON Temple
  • Prem Mandir
  • Nidhivan
  • Vrindavan parikrama by rickshaw
  • All meals
03
Day 03

Govardhan · The Hill He Lifted

22 kilometres of devotion

Drive to Govardhan (25km). Govardhan Hill — the hill that Krishna lifted on his little finger for seven days to protect Braj from Indra's deluge, in the Bhagavata Purana's most spectacular miracle. Devotees circumambulate the entire hill — 22km — as a parikrama, some prostrating the entire way. Mansi Ganga (the sacred lake at the base). Kusum Sarovar — one of the most beautiful tanks in Braj, built by Maharaja Suraj Mal in the 18th century.

Included Today
  • Govardhan Hill parikrama (partial or full)
  • Mansi Ganga
  • Kusum Sarovar
  • Govardhan temple
  • All meals
04
Day 04

Barsana · Radha Rani · Nandgaon

Radha's village

Drive to Barsana (45km). Barsana — Radha's birthplace and home, built on four hills representing the four gates of Brahma's lotus. The Radha Rani Temple on the hilltop is one of the most joyful temples in Braj — the energy here is different from the temples of Krishna, lighter, more playful. Nandgaon (15km) — Krishna's foster home with Nanda and Yashoda. The Nanda Bhavan temple on the hill. Return to Vrindavan.

Included Today
  • Drive to Barsana
  • Radha Rani Temple
  • Nandgaon Nanda Bhavan
  • Lathmar Holi (if timing aligns)
  • All meals
05
Day 05

Gokul · Departure

The first home

Morning drive to Gokul (15km from Mathura) — where Krishna was brought as an infant and where he spent his first years before moving to Vrindavan. The Thakurani Ghat where Vasudeva crossed the Yamuna with the infant Krishna. Transfer to Mathura station or Agra airport.

Included Today
  • Gokul and Thakurani Ghat
  • Airport or station transfer
  • Prasadam for your journey
